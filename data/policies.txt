Customer Support Policy & Knowledge Base

1. REFUNDS
- Refunds are only processed for items returned within 30 days.
- Electronics have a 15-day return window.
- Shipping costs are non-refundable unless the item was defective.
- To process a refund, the customer must provide the Order ID.

2. ORDER STATUS
- Standard shipping takes 5-7 business days.
- Express shipping takes 2-3 business days.
- Tracking numbers are emailed within 24 hours of shipment.
- If an order is "Processing" for more than 48 hours, check for payment issues.

3. TECHNICAL ISSUES
- For "Device Not Powering On": Ask customer to charge for 2 hours and try a different cable.
- For "Connection Errors": Reset the device by holding the power button for 10 seconds.
- If the issue persists, offer a replacement if under warranty (1 year).

4. URGENCY GUIDELINES
- High Urgency: Safety issues, billing errors > $100, complete service outage.
- Medium Urgency: Shipping delays, product defects, billing errors < $100.
- Low Urgency: General questions, feature requests, feedback.

5. TONE & STYLE
- Always be empathetic and professional.
- Use clear, concise language.
- Apologize for any inconvenience.
- Sign off with "Best regards, Customer Support Team".
